Feed Forward

About us

Feed Forward connects restaurants, grocery stores, and individuals with local food banks. We rescue high-quality surplus food and deliver it where it’s needed most, reducing waste and fighting food insecurity across the province.

We work with restaurants, grocery stores, and, individuals to create a sustainable safe network.

Donate Now 🫳🏻 I'm a Food Bank 🫴🏻

🌱 Our Mission

To connect restaurants, grocery stores, and, individuals with food banks through a dignified, tech-driven network ensuring no edible food goes to waste and no person goes hungry.

⭐ Our Vision

Our work connects people, food, and communities across Nova Scotia by expanding access to affordable, culturally appropriate food while addressing the root causes of food insecurity in the region.
